DCCN

DCCN (Data Communication and Computer Networks) is a subject that
deals with the principles and techniques of transmitting data between
computers and devices over various types of networks. It covers topics
like network architecture, protocols, transmission media, switching techniques,
and network security. Understanding DCCN is essential for designing, implementing,
and managing efficient and secure communication networks, such as the internet and local
area networks (LANs).
