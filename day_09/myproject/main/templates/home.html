MCA (Master of Computer Applications) is a postgraduate degree program
in computer science and applications. It typically spans 2 to 3 years,
depending on the country and university. The course is designed to provide
in-depth knowledge of areas like software development, computer networks,
database management, web technologies, and programming languages.
MCA is ideal for students who want to pursue careers as software developers,
system analysts, data scientists, or IT consultants. It combines both theoretical
knowledge and practical training to prepare students for the IT industry.
