AFM

Accounting and Finance Management involves the systematic
recording, analyzing, and reporting of financial transactions
of a business or organization. Accounting focuses on tracking income,
expenses, assets, and liabilities to provide accurate financial information.
Finance management uses this data to plan, control, and make decisions about
the allocation of resources, investments, budgeting, and risk management. Together,
they ensure the financial health and sustainability of an organization.
