E-Commers

AE-commerce (Electronic Commerce) refers to buying and selling
goods and services over the internet. It allows businesses and
consumers to conduct transactions online, making shopping faster
and more convenient. E-commerce includes various models like B2B
(business-to-business), B2C (business-to-consumer), and C2C
(consumer-to-consumer). It involves technologies like online payment
systems, digital marketing, and supply chain management, transforming
traditional retail and business operations.
